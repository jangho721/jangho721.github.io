I'm a researcher at TmaxEduAI, where I lead the design and development of personalized recommendation systems and AI-driven diagnostic models for student learning. My work focuses on building scalable, impactful solutions that leverage data-driven insights to enhance education outcomes.

Before that, I received MS. in Artificial Intelligence at Hanyang University where I was advised by Dr. Eunok Paek. My MS research focuses on addressing challenging problems in proteomics, a branch of bioinformatics, using machine learning techniques. I began my research journey at Inha University's Construction Systems Laboratory, where I worked on developing an object detection model to identify small- to medium-sized objects that could pose safety risks in indoor construction sites. My efforts were focused on tackling challenges related to real-time detection, lightweight model design, and adapting to indoor environments, aiming to create a practical and efficient safety monitoring solution.
